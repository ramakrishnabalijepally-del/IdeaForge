IdeaForge

An AI-powered platform for exploring startup and manufacturing business ideas — featuring semantic search, RAG-based AI generation, saved idea collections, and a full admin panel.

▶ View Live App 📄 GitHub Repository
Core Features
💡
Idea Explorer
Browse and filter curated startup & manufacturing ideas by category, feasibility, and capital range.
🤖
AI Idea Generator
Describe a problem and get a full structured business report — market, revenue model, roadmap, and tags.
🔍
AI Search (RAG)
Semantic vector search over the idea database using ChromaDB + Gemini embeddings with cited sources.
🌟
Saved Ideas
Bookmark and revisit ideas. Personalised recommendation engine surfaces related ideas on your dashboard.
🔒
JWT Auth
Secure httpOnly cookie-based auth with access + refresh token rotation and role-based access control.
📈
Admin Panel
Analytics dashboard, user management, contact submissions, and vector store reindex controls.
