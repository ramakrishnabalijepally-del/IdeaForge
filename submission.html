Core Features
💡
Idea Explorer
Browse & filter curated startup ideas by category, feasibility, and capital range.
🤖
AI Idea Generator
Describe a problem, get a full structured business report with roadmap and revenue model.
🔍
AI Search (RAG)
Semantic vector search over the idea database using ChromaDB + Gemini embeddings.
🌟
Saved Ideas
Bookmark ideas and get personalised recommendations on your dashboard.
🔒
JWT Auth
Secure httpOnly cookie auth with refresh token rotation and role-based access control.
📈
Admin Panel
Analytics, user management, contact submissions and vector store reindex controls.
